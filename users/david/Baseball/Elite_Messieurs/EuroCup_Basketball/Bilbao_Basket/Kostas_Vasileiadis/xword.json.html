After four seasons between LEB and LEB 2, Bilbao promoted to Liga ACB in 2004 by winning by 3\u20131 to Baloncesto Le\u00f3n in the LEB semifinals.\nBilbao spent ten seasons in the top tier of the Spanish basketball becoming finalist of the league in the 2010\u201311 season, where it lost in the finals to FC Barcelona by 3\u20130. In 2012, Bilbao played the EuroLeague and it was eliminated in the quarterfinals by CSKA Moscow, who won the series by 3\u20131.\nThe best era of the club finished in 2013, when it lost the 2013 Eurocup Final at Charleroi against Lokomotiv Kuban.\nOn 17 July 2014, the ACB excluded Bilbao Basket from the Liga ACB due to its financial trouble, but on 8 August, the Association was forced to retire the exclusion and finally Bilbao Basket remained in the Spanish top league.The club played four more seasons in Liga ACB until their relegation on 13 May 2018, after losing against Baskonia the
